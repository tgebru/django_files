I am a PhD student in the Stanford Artificial Intelligence Laboratory, studying computer vision under Fei-Fei Li. My main research interest lies in data mining large scale publicly available images to gain sociological insight. Stay tuned for related news coming soon. Prior to joining Fei-Fei's lab I worked at Apple designing circuits and signal processing algorithms for various Apple products including the first ipad.
